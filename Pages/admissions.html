Admission Process

How to Join London College Nansana

  1. Fill out the application form online or at the school office.
  2. Submit required documents (see Fees & Requirements).
  3. Attend an interview and assessment (if applicable).
  4. Receive admission decision and pay registration fee.
  5. Complete enrollment and join orientation.

For more details, contact our admissions office.

Class Termly Fees

📋 Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (copy)
  • Previous school report cards
  • Passport photos (4 pieces)
  • Medical certificate
  • Parent/Guardian ID copy
  • Registration fee: UGX 50,000

Scholarships

Supporting Academic Excellence

  • Merit Scholarships: Awarded to top-performing students in national exams.
  • Sports Scholarships: For outstanding athletes representing the school.
  • Need-Based Scholarships: For students from disadvantaged backgrounds.
