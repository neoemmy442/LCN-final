FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is the medium of instruction at London College Nansana?

A1: The medium of instruction is English. We provide additional language support for non-native speakers.

Q2: How can I apply for admission?

A2: You can apply online through our website or download the application form and submit it to the school office.

Q3: What are the school fees?

A3: Please refer to the Fees & Requirements section for detailed information on the fee structure.

Q4: Does the school offer scholarships?

A4: Yes, we offer merit-based and need-based scholarships. Please check the Scholarships section for more information.

Q5: What extracurricular activities are available?

A5: We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports, music, drama, and various clubs and societies.

Privacy Policy

How we handle information shared with the school

London College Nansana respects the privacy of students, parents, staff, and visitors. Personal details collected through forms or school communication channels are used only for academic, administrative, and support purposes.

  • We collect only the information needed for school services.
  • We do not share personal information without a valid reason or consent.
  • We protect records through appropriate administrative and technical safeguards.
  • Parents and guardians may contact the school office for clarification about stored information.
