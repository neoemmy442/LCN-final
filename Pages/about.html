Our History

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 2008, London College Nansana has grown from a small community school to one of the most respected educational institutions in the region.

🏗️ 2008 - Foundation

London College Nansana was established with just 50 students and 5 teachers, driven by a vision to provide quality education to the community.

📈 2012 - Growth

Expanded facilities and programs, introducing science laboratories and computer labs. Student population grew to 200.

🏆 2018 - Recognition

Achieved outstanding academic results and received recognition as one of the top schools in the district.

🎓 2024 - Excellence

Today, we serve over 500 students with 50+ qualified teachers, maintaining our commitment to educational excellence.

Mission & Vision

Our Guiding Principles

🎯 Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and moral character while preparing students for success in higher education and life.

🌟 Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution that nurtures global citizens who are academically excellent, morally upright, and socially responsible.

💎 Core Values

Excellence, Integrity, Respect, Innovation, Collaboration, and Community Service guide everything we do at London College Nansana.
