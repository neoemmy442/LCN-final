Curriculum

Comprehensive Education Program

📚 Ordinary Level (O-Level)

Senior 1-4: Core subjects including Mathematics, English, Sciences, Social Studies, and elective subjects preparing students for national examinations.

🎓 Advanced Level (A-Level)

Senior 5-6: Specialized combinations in Sciences, Arts, and Commercial subjects preparing students for university entrance.

💻 ICT Integration

Computer literacy and digital skills integrated across all subjects, with dedicated computer labs and internet connectivity.
